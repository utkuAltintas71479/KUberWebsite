About Us

We are a team of 5 senior students from the computer engineering department of Koç University who wanted to provide their peers an improved way of hitchhiking, and while doing so bringing the community closer together.

Easy to use.

Specially designed so that you won't miss on a ride while figuring how to use the app.

Only for Koç University Students

A safer way to hitchhike and share a ride.

Budget&Environmental Friendly

Both for hitchhikers and riders, it is a way to decrease your spendings and save the planet.

How to user Kuber

Step1: Sign up with your KU email account
Step2: Verify your account from the link that is sent to your email address

Hitchhike

Specify your starting point, destination and time, then search for a good ride.

Ride

Specify your starting point, destination time and an optional fee, then publish a ride.
